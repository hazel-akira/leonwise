Offerings at First Baptist Church of Kenya

At First Baptist Church of Kenya, we believe in the importance of giving back to God as an act of worship and stewardship. Your offerings support the various ministries and programs of our church, enabling us to spread the message of God's love and serve our community with compassion and excellence.
Why Give?
Giving is a fundamental aspect of our faith journey. Scripture teaches us the principles of generosity and stewardship, emphasizing the joy and blessing that come from giving back to God.
Malachi 3:10 (NIV) says: "Bring the whole tithe into the storehouse, that there may be food in my house. Test me in this," says the Lord Almighty, "and see if I will not throw open the floodgates of heaven and pour out so much blessing that there will not be room enough to store it."
When you give faithfully and cheerfully, you partner with us in fulfilling the mission and vision of our church, impacting lives and transforming communities for the glory of God.

How to Give
1.Via MPESA
- We have a paybill account for the offerings and tithe.
Our business number is 522000.
Use your name as the account name.
2. Online Giving
- We offer a convenient and secure online giving platform, allowing you to give your offerings from anywhere at any time. Simply visit our website and click on the "Give" button to make a one-time donation or set up recurring contributions. Your information is kept confidential, and transactions are processed securely.
3. In-Person Giving
- You can also give during our worship services by placing your offering in the designated offering baskets. We encourage you to pray over your gift and give with a joyful heart as an act of worship.
4. Mail-in Donations
- If you prefer to mail your offerings, you can send your contribution to the following address:

Please make checks payable to "First Baptist Church of Kenya" and indicate any specific designation or memo on the memo line.

Your Impact

Every contribution makes a difference! Your offerings support:

Ministries:

Funding various ministries and outreach programs that spread the gospel and meet the spiritual needs of individuals and families.

Missions:

Supporting local and global missions efforts, empowering missionaries and partners to share the love of Christ around the world.

Community Outreach:

Providing assistance to those in need, offering practical help and spiritual support to our community.
Thank you for your generosity and faithful giving. Together, we can make a positive impact and advance God's kingdom here on earth.

If you have any questions or need assistance with giving, please don't hesitate to contact us. We're here to help!

how to pay using M-Pesa

Via MPESA
1. Go to M-Pesa Menu on your phone
2. Select Pay Bill Option.
3. Enter the business number of FBCK; 522000.
4. Enter your name as the account name.
5. Enter the amount you are paying e.g 500.
6. Enter your M-Pesa PIN e.g 1234
7. Press OK
8. You will receive a confirmation message with the Transaction code and amount
9. You will receive a confirmation message from FBCK confirmation message after payment.
