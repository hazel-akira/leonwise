About Us

Welcome to First Baptist Church, committed to loving God, loving people, and serving our community. For over 100 years, we have been a beacon of hope and faith in the heart of our city.

Our mission is to glorify God by making disciples of Jesus Christ who are devoted to growing in faith, serving others, and sharing the gospel with our neighbors and the world.

We invite you to join us for worship, fellowship, and service as we seek to live out the love of Christ in our community and beyond. Explore our website to learn more about our ministries, upcoming events, and how you can get involved!
